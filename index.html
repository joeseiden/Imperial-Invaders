You play a lone X-Wing pilot ambushed by a squadron of TIE Fighters on a scouting mission for the Rebellion. Can you fight your way through the Imperial fighter squadron and escape to hyperspace?
Imperial Invaders is a browser based game inspired by the classic arcade game, Space Invaders. It was built using Vanilla JavaScript rendered on a single HTML5 canvas element .
